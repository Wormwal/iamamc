Create & Inspire

About

International Association of Minecraft Architects (IAMA) is a Discord-based Minecraft building and architectural forum founded by several passionate Minecraft builders on 7th November, 2016. The IAMA has evolved to become a vibrant Discord community of Minecraft builders, digital artists, and enthusiasts from all around the world, all with the common goal to create and inspire.

Vision

We here at the IAMA have two aims.

To Create. We aim to create a humble and friendly Discord-based Minecraft community, as well as to provide a platform for beginners, passionate builders, and players with their own Minecraft project to showcase.

To Inspire. We want other players to understand that given the determination, and perseverance, they too, can create the same level of builds as we do, as we all use the same building blocks. Minecraft is a game full of potential, and we here at the IAMA see it as a canvas, a tool for constructed art.

Forum

The IAMA is a Discord-based Minecraft building and architectural forum. Providing a platform for any Minecraft player to showcase their builds, as well as other talents, and also to socialize with other players.
