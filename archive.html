The Charter of the IAMA serves as the association's governing document. It outlines our aims and objectives, the processes of forum management and administration, the structure of the association, the Code of Conduct, as well as disciplinary procedures for members of staff.
The document also includes the following appendices:

  • Appendix I: The IAMA Weekly Showcase Programme
    Detailing what the Weekly Showcase Programme (WSP) is, and how it is to be operated, demonstrated formatting standards, and the roles and responsibilities of members of staff.
  • Appendix II: The IAMA Road Style Guide
    Recommended road and vehicle style guide for IAMA forum members. Based on Highways England's Design Manual for Roads and Bridges (DMRB)
  • Appendix III: Internal Guidelines
    Guidance and general principles for building Ex-BT brand guidelines.
  • Appendix IV: Marketplace Guidelines
    General idea of what is expected by Microsoft for those wishing to enter the Minecraft Marketplace.
